We believe that sustainability can make a positive contribution to our company’s long-term business success. Our green business is an enterprise that has a minimal negative impact, or potentially a positive effect, on the global or local environment, community, society, or economy.

CSR:-Corporate Social Responsibility Corporate Social Responsibility is not a new concept in India. It’s a corporate initiative to assess and take responsibility for the company’s effects on the environment and impact on social welfare. Creating opportunities for employees to participate in socially responsible initiatives. With heightened consumer awareness on environmental issues, companies need to take proactive steps in supporting green initiatives as a source of competitive advantage. Proactively supporting and undertaking green initiatives are now a part of global good governance practices.
